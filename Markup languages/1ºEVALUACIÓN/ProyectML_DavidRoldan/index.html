Get to know us

Paradise resort in the amazing Seychelles islands where you can disconnect and discover new experiences. Get to know yourself thanks to all the activities we offer and forget about the problems of everyday life.

We started as a small rural house and, little by little, we expanded our resort until we became what we have come to do today. We are a great example of self-improvement and all these years they have helped us to improve and improve to give the best help to our clients so that they feel comfortable at all times.

What will you find

By traveling to our resort you will be able to find moments to be with people if that is what you are looking for or spaces to be alone and enjoy yourself. We also have amazing beaches where you can hang out and forget about all your problems. In addition, on the island we have a very important national park for the Seychelles, which contains a huge variety of fauna.

Rooms

The resort room is spacious and bright, with large windows offering stunning views of the white sandy beach and crystal clear water. The furnishings are elegant and comfortable, with a king-size bed covered by a soft comforter and a sofa bed next to the window. The room also has a desk and chair, as well as a small fridge and a flat-screen TV mounted on the wall. The bathroom has a large shower and luxury bath products. The private balcony has a table and chairs where you can enjoy the beautiful scenery and the sound of the sea in the distance. Overall, the resort room is a perfect place to relax and enjoy the natural beauty of the surroundings.
